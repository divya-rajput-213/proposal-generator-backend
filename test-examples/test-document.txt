Business Proposal Request

We are looking to develop a mobile application for our food court business. The app should allow customers to:

1. Browse menus from different vendors
2. Place orders and make payments
3. Track order status in real-time
4. Collect loyalty points
5. Receive notifications about special offers

Key Requirements:
- User-friendly interface
- Fast performance
- Secure payment processing
- Multi-vendor support
- Admin dashboard for vendors

Budget: $50,000 - $75,000
Timeline: 6 months
Target audience: Young professionals and students

Please provide a comprehensive proposal including technical approach, timeline, and cost breakdown.
